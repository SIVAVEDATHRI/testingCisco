Cisco Testing Details

Learn about the various testing methods used in Cisco devices, including chamber testing for environmental conditions.

What is Cisco Testing?

Cisco testing involves various procedures to ensure that Cisco devices meet high-quality standards before being shipped to customers. This includes rigorous testing of hardware and software performance, as well as compatibility with other networking components. One important method is environmental chamber testing, which simulates extreme environmental conditions.

Chamber Testing in Cisco

Chamber testing is used to simulate harsh conditions such as extreme temperatures, humidity, and vibrations to ensure the Cisco hardware performs under real-world conditions. This ensures the devices will work in various environments, from data centers to outdoor installations.
